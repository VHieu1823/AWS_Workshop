In this report, the Worklog serves as a project management and progress-tracking tool. It not only records the activities that have been carried out, but also reflects the decision-making process, problem-solving approaches, and collaboration among team members. As such, the worklog enhances transparency and enables a more comprehensive evaluation of the project’s overall effectiveness.
