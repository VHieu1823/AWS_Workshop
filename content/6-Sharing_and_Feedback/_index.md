## General Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment is friendly and open. Everyone is quite supportive and willing to help when I have difficulties. I feel comfortable working here and can focus on my tasks. It would be even better if there were more team bonding activities so people could get to know each other more.

### 2. Mentor / Admin Support

My mentor is very supportive and explains things clearly whenever I do not understand something. I am also encouraged to try solving problems on my own instead of just being given answers, which helps me learn a lot. The admin team also supports well with procedures and documents, making everything easier.

### 3. Relevance to Academic Major

The work I did is quite related to what I studied at university. At the same time, I also got exposed to new practical knowledge that I had not learned before. This helped me connect theory with real-world application.

### 4. Learning & Skill Development

During the internship, I improved several skills such as working with AWS, teamwork, communication, and self-learning. I also learned how a real project is organized and how people work in a company environment.

### 5. Company Culture & Team Spirit

The company culture is positive. People respect each other and work together well. When there is urgent work, everyone helps each other to complete tasks on time. I feel like I am part of the team even though I am just an intern.

### 6. Internship Policies / Benefits

The company provides a basic internship allowance and flexible support when needed. Internal training sessions are also useful for improving my knowledge and skills.

## Additional Questions

### Most Satisfying Aspect

The most satisfying part is the supportive environment and the guidance from my mentor, which helps me learn a lot during the internship.

### Areas for Improvement

### Would I recommend this internship?

Yes, I would recommend it because the environment is good and there are many opportunities to learn real-world skills.
